The middle_interface function.

The function that finds the center and radius of the ball that contains the most charge points and is bounded within surface S with tolerance. Use an MC-SA approach. tight tol for interface, looser otherwise Tries to maximize points listed in indmax, bounded points will be returned in ind2

Parameters:
Pa vector of cartesian coordinates of all atoms in the molecule
arada vector of floating points of all atom radii in the molecule
SPa vector of coordinates of all MSMS surface points in the mol
NPa vector of all MSMS surface points in the molecule
IPinda vector of indices for each MSMS point at the interface
censa CPnt object for center location
Ra floating point for center radius
Nan integer for the CG sphere index
tolSPthe tolerance of how much the sphere can protrude from SES
tolIPthe interface tolerance only for contact situation
indmaxa vector of indices for all unbounded atoms
ind2a vector of indices bounded by created CG sphere
